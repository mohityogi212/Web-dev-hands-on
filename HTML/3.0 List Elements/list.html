Mohit's Recipe for the Best Crunhy Bhels with Samosa


Ingridients


For the dough:


  • 1 bowl puff rice
  • 2 pieces aloo samosas
  • 1/2 cup besan sev
  • 6-8 pieces small maida puris
  • 1 piece finely chopped onion
  • 1 piece finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup sweet / sour dates/ taramind red chutney (bhel chutney)
  • 1/2 cup spicy green chillies coriander chutney (bhel chutney)
  • 1/4 cup spicy garlic chutney(bhel chutney)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped tomato
  • 1/4 cup chana dal namkeen
  • as required Chat masala
  • 1/2 piece lemon
  • 1/4 cup pomegranate pearls
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green coriander leaves to garnish

Time and other things to keep in mind

  • 15 minutes
  • 2 servings
  • Lots of cleaning after finishing this taste bud activating yum yum meal

Instructions to prepare it :


  1. Step 1 Assemble all mentioned ingredients for making Samosa Bhel.
  2. In deep container add 3 Fistful puff rice. Add 1 tbsp finely chopped onion, 2 small maida puris crushed, little besan sev, chana dal namkeen, chat masala. Mix well.
  3. Add hand crushed aloo samosa in it
  4. Add 1 tbsp garlic chutney,2 tbsp spicy green chillies coriander chutney,3 tbsp red sweet and sour dates/ tamarind chutney. Mix gently. Squeeze lemon juice, sprinkle little chat masala. Mix well.
  5. Transfer it in serving plate. Garnish with pomegranate pearls, tomato pieces, besan sev,chana dal namkeen, chopped onion and coriander leaves.
